'Indolympix' is a unique technology-based platform to create a sports culture in India. Exp[x] is launching this flagship product in its maiden event , Noida City Olympics , for which exp(X) has received the patronage of the UP Olympic Association, which is affiliated to the Indian Olympic Association.
Using a technology-based platform, exp[x] is developing a competition and training based structure in different cities in India to identify and train grassroots level young talent and build on it to create the future sports champions.
Indolympix ,Noida 2016 will commence in April 2016 and will have the participation of over 20,000 students across 100 schools in 15 sports. The extravaganza will begin with a grand opening ceremony comprising of a half-marathon, entertainment shows, stalls and exhibitions and will provide a reach in almost all of Noida.

Mission

To evolve a culture of sports in India by bringing out a sportsperson in every child in India to create a substantial base of sporting talent and evolving the future sports champions of India by professional training of the talent, besides providing a defined career path and healthy professional growth opportunities to sportspersons and associated personnel.

Vision

To develop a platform to bring out sportspersons who can make India reach the top five of the Olympics medal tally by 2024, top three by 2028 and the top by 2032 and the platform be made big so that it develops into an alternative career option for lakhs of people
