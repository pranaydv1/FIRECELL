Who We are

Founded in 2015, Xponential Growth Ventures Private Limited [exp(x)] is a multi-sector venture promoted by entrepreneurs with experience in finance and general management, technology, event management, marketing and operations domains. Xponential Growth Ventures Private Limited is setting up a platform to develop sports from the grassroots levels in India and search the potential sports champions of the future in India. The company is looking at setting up a competition-based structure to evaluate sports talent at the school level, which will then be trained by professionals and made to compete at more competitive levels. This mechanism of training and competition is akin to the education system. Through a mix of inter-school, inter-cluster, inter-region and national-level sports meets, apart from open competitions, we basically provide every aspiring sports person in the country a chance to live up to her/his ambition and develop into a sports professional. Besides incentivising and encouraging school going boys and girls through competitions, monetary rewards, sponsorships and scholarships, this platform intends to take care of the entire sports career of these potential talents.

We aim to bring all the ingredients of a good sporting culture on a single platform: aspiring sports persons, schools, coaches and academies, physiologists, dieticians, and other ancillary professionals related to sports, and sponsors In doing so, our company is attempting to bring in a true sports culture in India, which has been the root cause of failure of the country to win medals in line with the proportion of its population and resources.
