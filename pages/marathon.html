NOIDA HALF MARATHON

RUN FOR CHAMPIONS

TRIBUTE TO OUR INDIAN OLYMPIANS

The objective of the marathon is to spread awareness on sports champions in India. While cricket has yielded several highly recognized sportspersons, the awareness on sportspersons who have brought glory to India in various international competitions such as Olympics, Asian Games, Commonwealth Games and other individual and team sports continues to be low. Due to this lack of awareness of sports idols for our youngsters, most of our potential young champions do not have a story to relate to in these sports.

With Xponential Growth Ventures organising Indolympix Noida 2016 in August 2016 amongst our school children, we believe it is important for our youngsters to know about our champions and their struggles to the top. This will give these sports heroes the necessary visibility as also the participants a source of inspiration to take up the sport and reach to the very top. In the light of this, we are organizing, as part of the marathon, a Championsâ€™ Run of 2 km, an exclusive run for our sports invitees, our sports champions of the past and the present. We will also honour our sports champions by giving them awards to recognise their tremendous contribution to the sport in the midst of great odds and difficulties. This will spread awareness of various sports and sportspersons and act as an inspiration to the participants of Indolympix Noida 2016. .
